Help call seniors during the COVID-19 crisis

@homepageSubtitle
As social distancing continues for many weeks to come, physical isolation could lead to a severe secondary crisis for home-bound seniors.

The Neighbor Network matches volunteers with seniors to call in New York City, building relationships that will bring joy and comfort to seniors and volunteers alike.

@homepageAboutHeader
About Neighbor Network

@homepageAboutCopy
The Neighbor Network is an initiative aiming to deploy volunteers to call seniors in New York City for the duration of the COVID-19 crisis. These calls help fight social isolation, connect older adults with needed resources, and build relationships that will benefit both seniors and volunteers.
